## Participants

## Materials
- Explanation of the scenario
- Questionnaire (can be filled in remotely)

## Questionnaire Design
- Control questions (ask the same question again later, just differently formulated)
- Objective questions (e.g. `according to this explanation, what is the most important feature?`)
- ~5 examples from the dataset, incl. explanations for A-B testing (Likert Scale)
- Put some examples as (bonus) slides!

## Analysis
- Drop non-serious responses (minimum time taken + control questions)
- Objective questions: Accuracy
- Subjective questions: Mann-Whitney U Test

## Ethical Issues
- Participation is voluntary
- We need to collect certain sensitive information (e.g. age, gender, country of origin, a socioeconomic indicator, special medical conditions) to confirm our findings aren't biased. State clearly at the start of the study that this information will only be used for research purposes and will be deleted after, and the first question will be “Do you give permission for us to collect sensitive information for research purposes”. We would also ask for permission to collect this information from the UM ethics committee, and data would be collected and stored on secure UM servers.
